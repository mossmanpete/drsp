DRSP

DRSP, which stands for decentralized r/place (our inspiration), is a decentralized, completely peer-to-peer, persistent virtual world. It's built with IPFS pubsub room and delta-crdts. CRDTs are Confilct-free Replicated Data Types, and are the reason everyone on DRSP ends up with the same world-state no matter what order updates are recieved in.
